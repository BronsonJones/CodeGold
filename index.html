Congratulations to Bronson Jones!

Our member, Bronson Jones, has won the 2019-20 election and has become this year's FBLA State Parliamentarian. He will serve alongside the other state officers for the 2020-21 year.

FBLA State Leadership Conference

Tennessee State FBLA hosts the State Leadership Conference (SLC) for all of it's members each year. At the SLC, Our local chapter competes in many different business-related competitions. While there, our members also attend workshops that aid in strengthening their leadership skills.
